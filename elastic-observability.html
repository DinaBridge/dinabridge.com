What we deliver

Observability that actually works in production.

Most observability implementations break under real load or drown teams in noise. We build systems that surface the signals that matter.

Elastic APM

Instrumentation, configuration, and tuning of Elastic APM across distributed services. Latency analysis, error tracking, and performance baselines.

Log pipeline architecture

Ingest pipeline design with Logstash, Fleet, and Beats. Parsing, enrichment, routing, and retention — built for scale and operational simplicity.

Distributed tracing

Trace correlation across services and infrastructure. We configure sampling strategies and trace context propagation for production environments.

Infrastructure monitoring

Host, container, and Kubernetes monitoring via Elastic Agent. Metric collection, anomaly detection, and SLO-based alerting.

Kibana dashboards

Purpose-built dashboards for SLO/SLA tracking, service health, and incident response. We build for operators, not demos.

Alert fatigue reduction

Threshold engineering, deduplication, and routing to reduce alert noise. We tune your alerting to surface incidents, not noise.

What we deliver

Keyword to semantic. Semantic to AI-native.

We help teams move from keyword-only search to semantic, vector-powered, and AI-augmented retrieval — without rebuilding everything from scratch.

ELSER Deployment

Elastic Learned Sparse EncodeR (ELSER) brings semantic understanding to Elasticsearch without the need for external embedding models. We handle deployment, index design, and tuning.

RAG Architecture

Retrieval-augmented generation pipelines that connect Elasticsearch as a knowledge base to your LLM layer. We design the retrieval side of RAG for accuracy, performance, and scale.

Vector Search

Dense vector search using kNN and approximate nearest neighbour queries. We design embedding pipelines, index mapping, and query strategies for your use case.

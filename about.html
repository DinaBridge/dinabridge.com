What makes a gem

Technical depth is the baseline.
Not the goal.

Soft skills

They read the room

They translate a production incident into a calm, clear conversation. They know when to go deep and when to step back. Clients feel safe — not just technically covered.

Ownership

They lead, not wait

They walk in, find the real problem beneath the stated one, and take it from there. No micromanagement needed. No hand-holding expected. They own the outcome.

Simplicity

They do not overcomplicate

Simple things should be simple. Complex things can be simplified. An overcomplicated architecture is a failure of clarity — not a sign of skill.
